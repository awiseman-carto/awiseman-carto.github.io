"Grunge" Map Tiles

For an assignment, I had to design map tiles for a fictional movie studio making a movie about the grunge musical movement. I used pictures of Soundgarden, Nirvana, Screaming Trees and Temple of the Dog for inspiration and designed this tile set. Try zooming into to Seattle or Aberdeen, Washington.
