Colombia Terrain

I designed a hypothetical route for a 21-stage cycling race as an exercise to make shaded relief and hillshade raster images. The route goes past my parents' and uncle's houses in Knoxville, passes the highest mountain in the eastern US (Mount Mitchell) and goes through other cool southern towns like Asheville, Boone, Abingdon, Harrisonburg and others.
